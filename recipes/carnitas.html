Crispy Carnitas

Description

Instant Pot Carnitas is a simple and easy way to make a delicious meal in less than 3 hours. Seasonings. Searing. Patience. That's all it takes to make this course.

Ingredients

  • 1 (4-5 lb) pork cut, trimmed to 2-inch chunks
  • Fine sea-salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive-oil
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tsp chili powder

Steps

  1. Pour oil into pressure cooker and set to saute.
  2. Mix dry ingredients into a medium sized mixing bowl.
  3. Cut pork into sizable chunks and mix in with seasonings.
  4. Sear meat for 4 minutes per side.
  5. Press "Cancel" to turn off heat.
  6. Pour in chicken broth.
  7. Close pressure cooker lid and set vent to "sealing".
  8. Cook on high pressure for 1hr, followed by 15 minutes of cooldown.
  9. (Optional) To get the meat extra crispy, strain the juice from the meat into a measuring cup.
  10. Place cooked pork on an oven tray evenly and pour 1/3 of the juice on top of the meat.
  11. Turn on your oven broiler to "High" heat.
  12. Place tray in the oven for 10 minutes.
  13. Remove tray from oven and stir the meat around. Soft meats facing up.
  14. Repeat until satisfied with crispiness.
  15. Serve with cilantro, lime and tortillas.
  16. Enjoy! And don't forget your cerveza.
